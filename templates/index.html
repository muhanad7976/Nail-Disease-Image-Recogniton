F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nail AI Diagnose?

Nail AI Diagnose is an AI-powered web platform that helps users identify nail diseases by uploading an image. It uses deep learning to analyze the image and provides accurate disease predictions along with treatment guidance.

How do I use the system?

Simply go to the website, click on the “Upload Image” button, and select a clear photo of your nail. The AI will process the image and display the diagnosis instantly along with useful information.

Which nail diseases can the system detect?

Currently, the system can detect multiple nail conditions, including Acral Lentiginous Melanoma, Onychogryphosis, Pitting, Bulging, and more. Each condition is supported by verified medical information to ensure accurate results.

Is the system accurate and safe to use?

Yes, the system uses a well-trained deep learning model with high accuracy. However, it is designed for educational and preliminary diagnostic use. Always consult a medical professional for final diagnosis or treatment.

Can I use the system on mobile devices?

Yes, Nail AI Diagnose is fully responsive and works on all modern smartphones, tablets, and desktop devices.

Is my uploaded image stored or shared?

No. Your uploaded image is not saved or shared. It is used temporarily for prediction and is deleted immediately after processing to ensure privacy and security.
